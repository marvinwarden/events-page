Flea Market

A flea market or swap meet is a type of street market that provides space for vendors to sell previously-owned (second-hand) merchandise. This type of market is often seasonal. However, in recent years there has been the development of 'formal' and 'casual' markets which divides a fixed-style market (formal) with long-term leases and a seasonal-style market with short-term leases.

Venue: V&A Waterfront

Time: 09:00

date: 24 July 1985
